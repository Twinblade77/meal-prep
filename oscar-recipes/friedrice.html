Shrimp Fried Rice

Easy and healthy shrimp fried rice recipe that is quick, easy, and delicious!
This recipe makes 5 plates.
(400 calories, 13g fat, 21g carbs, 45g protein) per plate

Ingredients

  • 3 Bags of Riced Cauliflower
  • 3 lb of Shrimp
  • 5 Brown Eggs
  • 1/2 cup of Soy Sauce
  • 1 Bag of Frozen Vegetable Medley
  • 2 Ginger Cubes
  • 2 Garlic Cubes
  • 1 Tbsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • Salt and Pepper

How to prepare:

  1. Heat a wok or large frying pan until water beads on the surface
  2. Add Cauliflower and gently toss until crispy
  3. Make a hole in the center of the rice and add beated eggs
  4. Cook eggs then mix with rice
  5. Add Frozen Vegetable Medley and mix with rice
  6. Add soy sauce mixture and toss
  7. Lastly add the shrimp and gently toss the entire mixture
  8. Remove wok from flame and allow to cool before separating into tupperware dishes
