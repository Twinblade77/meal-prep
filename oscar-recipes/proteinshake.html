Almond Milk Protein Shake

Easy and healthy almond milk protein shake when you are on the go.
(150 calories, 4g fat, 4g carbs, 25g protein)

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Almond Milk
  • 1 Scoop of whey protein

How to prepare:

  1. Add almond milk to a mason jar
  2. Add whey protein
  3. Shake vigorously for 30 seconds
  4. Enjoy!
